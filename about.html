About Us

Ittifaq Feeds Pvt Ltd is a leading supplier of high-quality poultry farm feed dedicated to supporting the poultry industry with nutritious and reliable products. Founded with the vision of empowering farmers and enhancing poultry productivity, we pride ourselves on delivering excellence through our innovative feed formulations and stringent quality control processes.

Our mission is to provide poultry farmers with top-grade feeds that promote healthy growth and maximize yields. We continuously invest in research and development to improve our products and adapt to the evolving needs of the industry. At Ittifaq Feeds, customer satisfaction and sustainable farming are at the heart of everything we do.

With a dedicated team of experts, modern manufacturing facilities, and a commitment to quality, we strive to be the trusted partner for poultry farmers across the region.
